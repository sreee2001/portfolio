The AI Sovereignty Crisis

Every Enterprise Faces This Problem

Companies are spending $200B+ annually on AI (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google), but sending sensitive data to third-party APIs creates catastrophic compliance and IP risks. Healthcare, financial services, government, and defense contractors cannot use public AI APIs due to HIPAA, SOC 2, FedRAMP, and data residency requirements.

The result? Enterprises either avoid AI entirely (losing competitive advantage) or violate compliance policies by shadow IT usage of ChatGPT/Claude.
