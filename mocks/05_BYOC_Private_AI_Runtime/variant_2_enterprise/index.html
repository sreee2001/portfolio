Production-Grade AI Infrastructure

BYOC Enterprise deploys a fully managed AI runtime in your cloud environment with enterprise-grade reliability, security, and compliance controls. Our architecture is battle-tested at Fortune 500 companies processing 10B+ tokens/month.

Core Components:

  • Multi-Region Deployment: Active-active architecture across 3+ availability zones with automatic failover
  • GPU Cluster Management: Auto-scaled NVIDIA A100/H100 instances with spot instance optimization (60% cost savings)
  • High-Availability Load Balancing: Global traffic routing with health checks, circuit breakers, and rate limiting
  • Model Serving Layer: vLLM, TensorRT-LLM, or TGI with batching, caching, and sub-200ms P99 latency
  • Security & Compliance: Private VPC, WAF, DDoS protection, encryption at rest/transit, and compliance automation
  • Monitoring & Observability: Prometheus, Grafana, distributed tracing, anomaly detection, and incident management

Deployment Model: Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform/CloudFormation) with GitOps workflows. Automated updates, blue-green deployments, and rollback capabilities. Your DevOps team has full visibility and control.
