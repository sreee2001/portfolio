🚀 GETTING STARTED

Build Your First Workflow

Follow our proven 4-step process to automate any Slack workflow in minutes

Choose Your Trigger

Start by selecting what kicks off your workflow. Pick from dozens of Slack events or external app triggers.

  • Slack message, emoji reaction, channel join
  • Time-based triggers (daily, weekly, custom schedule)
  • Webhook triggers from external systems
  • Form submissions and button clicks

Design Your Workflow

Use our drag-and-drop builder to connect actions, add conditions, and create branching logic visually.

  • Drag-and-drop action blocks (send message, update database)
  • Conditional logic with if/then/else branches
  • Variable mapping and data transformation
  • Real-time workflow preview as you build

Connect Your Apps

Integrate with 500+ apps and services. Authenticate once, then pass data seamlessly between systems.

  • Pre-built connectors for Google, Microsoft, Salesforce
  • Custom API integrations with OAuth support
  • Database connections (PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB)
  • Secure credential storage and management

Test & Deploy

Run test executions, review detailed logs, then deploy to your team with confidence.

  • One-click test runs with sample data
  • Detailed execution logs and error handling
  • Version control and rollback capabilities
  • Performance analytics and usage metrics
