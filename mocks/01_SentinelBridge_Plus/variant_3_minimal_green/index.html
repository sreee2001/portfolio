Traditional email-to-ticket workflows rely on manual sorting, leading to delays, mis-assignments, and duplicate work. SentinelBridge+ bridges the gap between your support inbox and ServiceNow with intelligent automation that learns from your team's patterns.

Instant Classification

Our LLM-powered engine reads email intent, extracts metadata, and assigns the correct category, urgency, and assignment group in seconds—eliminating the first touchpoint bottleneck.

Thread Intelligence

Advanced correlation algorithms detect related emails and prevent duplicate ticket creation, maintaining clean SLA tracking and reducing noise for your analysts.

Seamless Integration

Connects directly to Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and ServiceNow via secure OAuth. Teams notifications keep everyone in sync without leaving their workflow.

Privacy First

Deploy in our cloud or yours (BYOC). Enterprise-grade SSO, RBAC, and audit logs ensure compliance with your security policies.

Continuous Learning

Classification models improve over time by learning from corrections and feedback, adapting to your organization's unique language and ticket patterns.

Actionable Metrics

Real-time dashboards track mean-time-to-acknowledge, classification confidence, agent workload, and SLA adherence—giving leadership clear visibility.
