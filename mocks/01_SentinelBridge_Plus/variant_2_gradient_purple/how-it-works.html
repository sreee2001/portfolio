How SentinelBridge+ Works

Four simple steps to transform your helpdesk operations

1

Connect Your Mailbox

Authorize SentinelBridge+ to monitor your support mailbox (e.g., support@yourcompany.com) via Microsoft Graph API or IMAP. OAuth 2.0 ensures secure, read-only access. Setup takes under 5 minutes.

2

AI Classification Engine

Each incoming email is analyzed by our fine-tuned LLM classifier. It extracts intent, urgency, category (incident/request/question), and suggests assignment groups based on historical patterns and your custom rules.

3

ServiceNow Integration

Classified emails are automatically converted to ServiceNow tickets via REST API. Thread correlation prevents duplicates, and confidence scores let you set thresholds for auto-creation vs. human review.

4

Real-Time Notifications

New tickets trigger adaptive cards in Microsoft Teams channels. Analysts can approve, reassign, or escalate directly from Teams. Metrics dashboard tracks MTTA reduction and classification accuracy.
