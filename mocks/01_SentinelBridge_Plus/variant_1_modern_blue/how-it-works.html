How SentinelBridge+ Works

Four simple steps to automate your IT helpdesk triage

The Process

1

Connect Your Support Mailbox

Authorize SentinelBridge+ to access your support email inbox (e.g., support@company.com) via secure OAuth 2.0 connection. We support Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and IMAP. Setup takes less than 5 minutes with our guided wizard.

2

AI-Powered Email Analysis

Our fine-tuned LLM analyzes each incoming email to extract key information: issue type (incident/request/question), urgency level, affected service, and suggested assignment group. The model learns from your historical tickets to improve accuracy over time.

3

Automatic Ticket Creation

Classified emails are automatically converted into ServiceNow tickets using REST API integration. Our intelligent thread correlation engine detects related emails to prevent duplicate tickets, maintaining clean ticket history and accurate SLA tracking.

4

Team Notifications & Monitoring

New tickets trigger real-time notifications in Microsoft Teams channels via adaptive cards. Your team can review, approve, or reassign tickets directly from Teams. The analytics dashboard tracks mean-time-to-acknowledge (MTTA), classification accuracy, and team performance metrics.
